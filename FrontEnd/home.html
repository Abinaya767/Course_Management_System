About Our Platform

The Student Course Management and Learning Progress Tracking System (SCMS) is a centralized digital learning platform designed to simplify academic management for both students and administrators.

Students can easily register, enroll in courses, access learning materials, and track their progress in real-time. Each course is divided into structured modules, allowing learners to complete topics step-by-step in an organized manner.

The system also provides a personalized dashboard where students can view their enrolled courses, completed modules, and overall progress percentage, helping them stay motivated and focused.

For administrators, the platform offers powerful management tools to create and update courses, manage student data, monitor enrollments, and generate academic reports efficiently.

This system ensures a smooth, user-friendly, and structured learning experience, making education more accessible and organized in a digital environment.
